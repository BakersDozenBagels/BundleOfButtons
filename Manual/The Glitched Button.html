On the Subject of The Glitched Button

This is a button. It is...?

The module contains a glitched button and a screen consisting of white 0's and 1's.

The displayed sequence will be found in the table below. However, one bit has been flipped.

Tap the button when the seconds digits of the timer are equal to the corresponding number, or are 30 higher than it.
